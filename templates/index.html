SCHOLAR MAKER

What is it?

Scholar Maker creates and shares beautiful lesson plans. In just a few minutes you can create lesson plans and share them as a website or as an Adobe PDF file.

Why do I care?

It's easy to learn in groups when you have a lesson plan. Scholar Maker quickly creates that lesson plan and allows you to share it with your group.

How do I use Scholar Maker?

Get Started! There's no need to log in! Or see a list of existing lessons first.
